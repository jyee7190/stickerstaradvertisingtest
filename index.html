01 — About Us

Fifteen years of putting Lahad Datu on the wall, the van, and the shopfront.

Sticker Star Advertising has operated out of Bandar Sri Perdana since 2010 — a Bumiputera-owned business built around one large-format print floor and a small, hands-on team. We handle the full job in-house, from the first design file to the final install, so what you see on our workshop floor is exactly what goes up on your wall, your van, or your shopfront.

Our client list runs from single shoplot owners to government departments, agencies and schools across the district — which means every job, big or small, gets the same production standard.
